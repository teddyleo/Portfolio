I AM

Chloe Wu

Born in Sydney, Australia and raised in Singapore, Chloe has always had a love for performing. Be it acting, singing or dancing, any chance she had to be in front of a crowd, you would find her there.

In 2007, Chloe moved to Tokyo, Japan to pursue her career as a performer. There, she started to focus more on her singing, and officially became a singer-songwriter when her music became available on the iTunes Music Store. She has also done several collaborations with internationally renowned EDM artist, IGODA.

To further pursue her passion, Chloe has moved to Los Angeles, California. She has trained for two years as an actress at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, and also in the Acting Intensive Program at the South Coast Repertory Theater, one of the largest equity theaters on the West Coast. Chloe hopes to continue not only singing, but also acting.
